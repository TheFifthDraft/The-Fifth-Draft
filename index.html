How I work

Five drafts.
One masterpiece.

Every project goes through the same rigorous process. The name isn't a gimmick — it's a commitment to never settling for good enough.

Draft 01

Discover

I get inside your brand, your customers, and your competitors. Deep listening before a single word is written.

Draft 02

Structure

Messaging hierarchy, narrative arc, and argument flow. I decide what to say before I decide how to say it.

Draft 03

Write

The first full draft — rough edges and all. I don't polish before I've got the raw material right.

Draft 04

Refine

I cut, sharpen, rearrange. This is where good becomes great. Where the obvious becomes the unexpected.

Draft 05

Perfect

The final read. Every word earns its place. Nothing left in that shouldn't be. Nothing missing that should.
